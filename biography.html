Meet our MD/CEO

“A life dedicated to service is the greatest biography one can ever write.”

~ Hon. Monday Diamond Ani, JP (Odozi Obodo)

Hon. Monday Diamond Ani, JP, popularly known as Odozi Obodo, is a distinguished Nigerian public administrator, accomplished youth leader, philanthropist, media strategist, and community development advocate. Renowned for his visionary leadership and unwavering commitment to grassroots empowerment, he has built an impressive career spanning public service, youth development, governance, entrepreneurship, media, and humanitarian initiatives.

A multiple award-winning personality, Hon. Ani is widely recognized as a creative genius, veteran Master of Ceremonies, media expert, influencer, brand ambassador, event consultant, entrepreneur, and social worker. Throughout his career, he has consistently championed policies and programs that promote youth inclusion, peacebuilding, good governance, and sustainable community development.

He currently serves as the General Manager/Chief Executive Officer of the Enugu State Community and Social Development Agency (CSDA), following his appointment by His Excellency, Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah, Executive Governor of Enugu State. In this role, he oversees the implementation of strategic rural development initiatives aimed at improving the quality of life in underserved communities. Under his leadership, the Agency has delivered impactful interventions, including solar-powered water facilities, improved sanitation systems, and vocational empowerment tools for thousands of beneficiaries across Enugu State. His leadership style emphasizes transparency, accountability, data-driven decision-making, and active community participation.

Hon. Ani has held several notable leadership positions throughout his distinguished career. He is a former President of the Students' Union Government (SUG), Institute of Management and Technology (IMT), Enugu; former Chairman of the Council of SUG Presidents in Enugu State and the South East; former Chairman of the National Association of Nigerian Students (NANS) Presidential Special Taskforce on COVID-19 in Enugu State; former Leader of the Nigeria Corps Members Forum; Founding President of the Nkanu Youth Council; and former Special Assistant to the Governor of Enugu State on Culture and Tourism. He also serves as the National Vice President of the IMT Alumni Association.

Beyond public service, Hon. Ani has established and continues to lead several impactful organizations. He is the Executive Director of the Monday Diamond Foundation, a non-governmental organization committed to humanitarian service and community development; Director General of the Peace and Good Governance Group (P3G); Director General of the Kwado Peter-Mbah Movement (KPM); Chief Executive Officer of Odoziobodo Nigeria Limited; and the Founder of Enugu Weekly Bulletin and Global Update News, media platforms dedicated to information dissemination and public engagement.

An accomplished convener and advocate for youth empowerment and intellectual development, Hon. Ani has successfully organized numerous high-impact programs, including the IMT Debate (2015, 2016, and 2018), Coal City Debate (2019), South East Debate (2021), National Debate (2022), Nkanu Unity Cup (2022), and the Youth Security Summit (2023). These initiatives have provided platforms for leadership development, civic participation, and youth engagement across Nigeria.

Academically, Hon. Monday Diamond Ani is an alumnus of both the Institute of Management and Technology (IMT), Enugu, and the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN). He holds advanced qualifications, including a Master's Degree and a Doctorate in Public Administration and Local Government, reflecting his commitment to scholarship and effective public governance.

His contributions to humanitarian service and community development have earned him several recognitions. In 2026, he received the prestigious Apostle of Faith Award from the Methodist Church Nigeria in recognition of his outstanding humanitarian interventions and unwavering commitment to societal development. He has also represented Enugu State on national platforms, including strategic engagements with the NG-CARES Programme in Abuja, where he has worked to strengthen partnerships for poverty reduction, economic empowerment, and sustainable development.

Driven by an enduring passion for service, Hon. Monday Diamond Ani continues to inspire a new generation of leaders through visionary governance, impactful philanthropy, youth advocacy, and transformative community development. His life's work reflects an unwavering dedication to building stronger institutions, empowering communities, and creating lasting opportunities for future generations.

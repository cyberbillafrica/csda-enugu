About CSDA Enugu

Community-driven development for a better tomorrow

Who We Are

The Community and Social Development Agency (CSDA), Enugu State, is a government agency committed to improving the standard of living of communities through social intervention, empowerment programs, and infrastructure development.

Our mandate focuses on reducing poverty, empowering vulnerable groups, and supporting sustainable development across all local government areas in Enugu State.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable community development and economic empowerment through strategic interventions and inclusive social programs.

Our Vision

To build resilient communities where every citizen has access to opportunities, support systems, and improved livelihoods.

Our Core Values

Transparency

Open and accountable processes in all our operations.

Accountability

Responsible management of resources and public trust.

Inclusion

Ensuring equal access and opportunities for all communities.

Sustainability

Creating lasting impact through long-term development solutions.

Leadership

Under the leadership of Hon. Monday Diamond Ani, CSDA Enugu continues to expand opportunities for empowerment, strengthen community participation, and improve public service delivery.

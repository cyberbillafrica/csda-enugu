Official Welcome

Welcome to CSDA Enugu

The Community and Social Development Agency (CSDA), Enugu State, is committed to improving the quality of life of our people through inclusive community development, empowerment initiatives, and sustainable support programs for vulnerable citizens.

Through strategic interventions, vocational empowerment, and financial support schemes, we continue to create opportunities that strengthen livelihoods and promote economic growth across communities in Enugu State.

On behalf of the agency, I warmly welcome you to this empowerment initiative. Together, we are building opportunities for our people and creating a brighter tomorrow.

— Hon. Monday Diamond Ani

GM/CEO, Enugu State CSDA

Biography

Hon. Monday Diamond Ani, JP, popularly known as Odozi Obodo, is a distinguished Nigerian public administrator, accomplished youth leader, philanthropist, media strategist, and community development advocate...

He currently serves as the General Manager/Chief Executive Officer of the Enugu State Community and Social Development Agency (CSDA)...

Academically, Hon. Ani is an alumnus of both IMT Enugu and UNN, holding advanced qualifications including a Master's Degree and Doctorate in Public Administration and Local Government...

Driven by an enduring passion for service, Hon. Monday Diamond Ani continues to inspire a new generation of leaders through visionary governance, impactful philanthropy, youth advocacy, and transformative community development.
